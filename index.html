How we calculated this
Payroll & statutory — ~6 min/employee/month for calculation + separate portal submissions to EPF (i-Akaun), SOCSO/EIS (PERKESO), PCB (e-PCB), and HRDCorp. 4 portals × monthly = significant overhead. Annual filing (Form EA, Form E) adds to year-end load.
Leave management — ~5 min/employee/month: receiving applications, manager approval routing, balance tracking, medical cert verification, carry-forward calculations. Scales linearly with headcount.
Claims & expenses — ~12 min/employee/month: paper/email receipt collection, verification, approval chain, data entry, reimbursement processing. Highly manual without a system.
Time & attendance — ~10 min/employee/month: timesheet collection, OT classification (normal day / rest day / public holiday under EA 1955), reconciliation before payroll lock. Errors require rework.
Onboarding — ~3 hrs HR effort per new hire (contract prep, document collection, statutory registration, system setup, orientation). Based on industry benchmarks for SMEs.
Digital reduction — 85–92% reduction per task based on published case studies. Conservative vs. vendors who claim 95%+. Residual digital time accounts for review, exceptions, and approvals that still require human judgment.
BrioHR Estimates are indicative. Actual savings depend on current process maturity.
